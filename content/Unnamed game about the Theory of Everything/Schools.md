Each player is a pupil in their school of theory. Schools are split into 3 categories. Most have an opposite (see if you can guess which is which!)

**Psychological** - The phenomena that effects the conscious mind and are mentally felt.
- [[Embodiment of Emotion]]- Study of emotional states and their pros and cons to performance.
- [[Embodiment of Faith]]- Study of instincts, willpower, and belief.
- [[Embodiment of Logic]]- Study of.. studying! Putting those puzzle pieces together and filtering data.
- [[Embodiment of Zen]]- Study of mental control and perception.

**Physical** - The world we experience through our senses!
- [[Study of Material]]- Study of matter, refining, physical mechanics.
- [[Study of Energy]]- Study of *states* of matter, wavelengths, and particle science.
- [[Study of Gravity]]- Study of gravity, and how it redirects energy through space.
- [[Study of Space]] - Study of location, dimensions, and geometry.

**Metaphysical** - The forces in the universe that cannot be physically touched, cannot be mentally felt, but can be *experienced*. (These schools are typically support schools, instead of primary)
- [[Aspect of Time]] - Study of relativity and its effects on *everything* .
- [[Aspect of Cycles]] - Study of patterns and loops in the universe.
- [[Aspect of Balance]] - Study of Entropy and natural balances.
- [[Aspect of Abstract]] - Study of the unknown, unexplainable, and unexpected.

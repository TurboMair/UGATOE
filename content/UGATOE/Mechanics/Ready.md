Ready dx "Stat" | Roll the "dx" dice equal to your "Stat" and add the total together, then subtract your "Stat" from the total. *(Ready Speed will not show a dice, instead use the dice shown in your stats)*

Ready dx **Pure** "Stat" | The same as above, but do not subtract your "Stat" from the total

Ready Pip | A shortcut for "Ready d2 Pure Magic. If less than or equal to 0, instead it is 1"
In simpler terms, flip coins (or roll a dice for Evens/Odds) equal to your magic stat. If you lose them all, you still gain 1 pip
*Note: Having a Magic of 1 means you will always gain only 1 pip, so you don't need to Ready!*

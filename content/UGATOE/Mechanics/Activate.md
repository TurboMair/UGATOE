#Mechanic 
Activate "game piece" | Turn/Reveal the game piece (or top most piece) face up. Begin any "Activated" triggers.
Deactivate "game piece" | Turn/Hide the game piece (or top most piece) face down. 

Activate a card from hand - Pay costs, Play it face up for its effect, discard when resolved. 
(Note: Action cards resolve after playing, before activating another card. Attack cards have a specific step when they resolve!)

Activated "game piece" - Any "game piece" that is face up and on the top of its pile (if it has one)

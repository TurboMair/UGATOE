1. **Plan & Ready**
	- [[Plan]] - Pull 3 [[Action Cards]] (They dictate what options you have for this turn. Such as Shop, Speak, Rest)
	- [[Ready]] - Roll [[Movement]]
2. **Move & Act**
	- **Move** - Use your entire movement pool and decide where to end your turn. (Empty space = field enemy).
	- **Act** - Chose your Action for the turn. (You may ask another player to [[Assist]] to provide an action card for you from their deck, but negotiations for payment are up to them!)
3. **Set**
	- Play any [[Boon & Bane]] effects
4. **Resolve** 
	- Pass the [[Turn Puck]] 
	- Begin [[Combat]] or resolve your [[Action Cards]]. 
		- While the next person starts their turn, the previous player before you puppets your enemy/actions if necessary. 

# Off Turn Structure
Just because it's not your turn, doesn't mean you can't interact!
- Assistance or roadblocks through **Flash** [[Boon & Bane]] effects.
- Perform deals with other players to [[Assist]] them.
- Puppet Master - If the player after you is resolving combat or complex actions, you may need to play puppet master. Your job is mostly handling RNG aspects! (Advanced playgroups may use an honor system to have players be their own puppet master)

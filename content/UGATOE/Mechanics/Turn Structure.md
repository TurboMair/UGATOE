1. **Plan & Ready**
	- [[Plan]] - Pull 3 [[Action Card]]s and use any Free Actions
	- [[Ready]] Speed for Movement
2. **Move & Act**
	- **Move** - Use your entire Movement and decide where to end your turn. *(If you cannot use your entire Movement, instead use as much as you can.)*
	- **Act** - Chose your Action for the turn. *(You may ask another player to [[Assist]] to provide an action card for you from their deck, but negotiations for payment are up to them!)*
3. **Set & Clean**
	- **Set** - Activate [[Boon & Bane]] effects *(Players may only control 1 Boon/Bane each)*
	- **Clean** - Return and shuffle your cards in hand to their deck.
4. **Pass & Resolve** 
	- Pass the [[Turn Puck]] 
	- Begin [[Combat]] or resolve your Activated [[Action Card]]s. 
		- While the next person starts their turn, the previous player before you puppets your enemy/actions if necessary. 

# Off Turn Structure
Just because it's not your turn, doesn't mean you can't interact!
- Perform deals with other players to [[Assist]] them. *(If you are in combat, you cannot assist. The active player can wait for the previous players to resolve combat to ask for assistance.)*
- Puppet Master - If the player after you is resolving combat or complex actions, you may need to play puppet master. Your job is mostly handling RNG aspects! *(Advanced playgroups may use an honor system to have players be their own puppet master)*

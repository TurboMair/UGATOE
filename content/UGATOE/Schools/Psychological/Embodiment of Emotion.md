#School 
Barbarian?
Buffs based on current emotion (Emotion tracker unique to this class). Tool box sort of playstyle, however emotion is effected from world actions. Burn out mechanic?

Opposite of [[Embodiment of Zen]]

Burnout begins at the start of the turn. If you Burnout during your turn, activate the effect then.

Emotion Step - End of turn;
	Roll the Action Die:
		1: Remove an Emotion
		2-6: Nothing
		7: Feel - (*Remove die if below 1*)
		8: Feel + (*If at max, nothing happens*)
		9: Shift (*Reroll an Emotion. If you have none, roll one and set it to Feel 1*)
		10: Burnout (*Activate the burnout effect of a current Emotion either now or next turn. X is still the current level*)
	You may remove any Emotion that Burned out,
### Emotions
Joy
	Plan: Draw +X Cards
	Burnout: Draw: Mill (*Whenever you draw, mill that many cards after. You may chose either between each draw or all at once after*)
Trust
	Boon: When Assisting, both players gain X gold
	Burnout: Bane: Only the Owner may Assist
Fear
	HP -X (*The max HP, this is not damage*)
	Burnout: Remove Fear and X number of status, Heal X+2
Surprise
	When rolling an Action or Attack, roll a separate d8. Crit Up: +/- X to your roll
	Burnout: Combat: Gain X Auto-Fizzle status
Sadness
	-X Movement result
	Burnout: Gain X Avoid status (*You ignore encounters*)
Disgust
	Gain Illness status if you don't have one (*Each turn, roll {some sort of stat check}. On Crit Down, 1 damage. on Crit Up, remove Illness. Illness is not removed at end of turn*). Disadvantage X on Illness roll.
	Burnout: Gain X Immune status (*You cannot gain any status on top of this. This does not remove previous status*), Remove Disgust and Illness
Anger
	When shuffling, +X Fizzle to deck (*Use Anger denoted fizzle cards. Remove these at end of encounter.*)
	Burnout: Gain X {Extra action name} status.
Focus
	Plan: Reveal X of deck, put 1 on the bottom. (*You may reorder them*)
	Burnout: Plan: Discard X randomly before drawing.

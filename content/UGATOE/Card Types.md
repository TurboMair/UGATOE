[[Action Cards]] - Action Deck used during the [[Plan]] and **Act** phase. 
- **Free Actions** - Cards that resolve during the Plan phase. These are useful to expand the Action cards you can chose from, or possibly generate resources.
[[Attack Cards]] - Attack Deck used during the [[Combat]] phase.
- **Trigger Cards** - Cards that trigger on draw, both good and bad.
[[Player Cards]] - Cards that attach and effect the Player (Not to be confused with [[Player Traits]])
- [[Gear Cards]] - Attached to the player character until removed
- [[Status Cards]] - Special cards with turn-based mechanics
**Active Cards** - Any face-up uncovered card you own (Including discard)
